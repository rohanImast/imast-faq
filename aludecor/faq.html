FAQ's

What is C&S Electrician loyalty program?

C&S Electrician loyalty program is for enrolled Electrician, with this program, the enrolled Electrician will be rewarded with rewards for recommending and getting sales done for C&S.

Is there any Enrolment fee?

No there is no enrolment fees.

How to approach C&S to get enrolled in this program?

There are three ways to enrolled yourself in this program:
• Mobile App
• C&S Sales Staff
• Contact No. 080-69081273

How to download the C&S aanand Electrician loyalty mobile application?

The application is available on Google Play Store for android phone users and IOS app store for IOS users.

Is there any further formality to get enrolled in C&S program?

Yes, there is a basic process for KYC validation, which is mandatory. To complete KYC process successfully, Following documents are required : • Any Photo ID (Aadhar Card, PAN Card & GSTIN)

What are redemption options available for the members?

Members can get cash rewards via Bank transfer or UPI transfer

How do I get benefit being a C&S program members?

On every scan of QR code pasted on C&S product packing you will be rewarded with points, These accumulated points can be redeemed against cash rewards.

When the Points can be redeemed?

At one's convenience

What is the helpline number or customer care number for the C&S aanand Electrician Loyalty program?

Our helpline number is 08069081273 (10:00 AM to 6:00 PM) Monday to Saturday

How can I change my mobile number?

In case of change in mobile number ,please contact our support centre 08069081273. Mobile number once provided at the time of registration cannot be changed, if required, old electrician ID has to be nullified and new electrician ID has to be created with carry forward of the points/ rewards
