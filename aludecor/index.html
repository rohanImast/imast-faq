About

Aludecor

Aludecor Engineers is leading automobile accessories manufacturing industry with An IATF 16949:20ISO 9001:2015 & 14001:2004 certified accessories manufacturer with an experience of more than 20 years. A strong workforce of more than 500 employees with 20 engineers specialized in production, development, marketing, and design. Aludecor has an inhouse blow molding, injection molding facility, and Polyurethane foam injection facility under one roof with a Vertical machining center. All accessories are designed considering RTO rules, Radiator clearance, and Light clearance. Automated systems to handle Orders and Dispatch in order to facilitate on-time deliveries. OEM supplier to Mahindra & Mahindra Ltd., Tata Motors Ltd. & Force Motors
